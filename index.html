ABOUT ME

Front End Web Developer/Georgia Peach


I am an Atlanta born and bred Front End Web Developer with experience in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Visual Design.

I am a former emergency room/trauma flight nurse who fell in love with learning to code after attending General Assembly's FEWD and Visual Design course. I am drawn to the blend of the technical and the creative and enjoy transforming complex problems into creative and visually aesthetic web designs. I bring my penchant for flexibility, years of team work collaboration experience, outstanding communication skills, and my ability to adapt and work under pressure to each project.

When I’m not developing websites for clients, or hard coding my portfolio site, you can find me being a mommy, a nurse, or both.
Whether you need a new website, or a hand with your existing content, I can help.
